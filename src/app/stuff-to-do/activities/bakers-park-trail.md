Charles Baker came to the confluence of Mineral, Cement and the Animas in the lead up to the Civil War. His account of the possible prospecting was nuanced but the legend grew and led to the <a href="https://coloradoencyclopedia.org/article/san-juan-county#The-San-Juan-Humbug-">San Juan Humbug</a>. The budding trail system that gives a nod to the pioneering crew offers an awesome view and easy access.

More info here on the single track society's website: <a href="https://www.silvertonsingletracksociety.org/bakerspark">https://www.silvertonsingletracksociety.org/bakerspark</a>
